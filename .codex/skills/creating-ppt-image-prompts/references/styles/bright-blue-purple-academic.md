# Bright Blue-Purple Academic

## Identity

Chinese name: 白底蓝紫明亮科技学术风

Use for academic reports, research proposals, postdoctoral presentations, technical reviews, and public-safety or AI research topics that need a bright, professional technology aesthetic.

## Best For

Academic reports, research proposals, postdoctoral presentations, technical reviews, and public-safety or AI research topics that need a bright, professional technology aesthetic.

## Canvas and Mood

- 16:9 landscape
- pure white background
- bright, airy, professional, academic
- generous whitespace
- flat or lightly dimensional illustration
- avoid large dark backgrounds and heavy shadows

## Palette Direction

- deep blue heading: `#173B8F`
- dark blue-black body: `#15213D`
- bright blue: `#2878F0`
- cyan accent: `#22B8CF`
- primary purple: `#7357E8`
- pale purple: `#B7A7F7`
- pale blue surface: `#EEF5FF`
- warning red: `#E84B5B`
- outcome green: `#35A66F`
- emphasis orange: `#F3A43B`

Treat hex colors as direction, not guaranteed exact rendering.

## Typography Direction

Use a modern Chinese sans-serif/Heiti appearance. Keep body text dark and high contrast. Use semibold module headings and regular body copy. Prefer shorter copy over tiny text.

## Visual Language

Use restrained combinations of:

- neural-network nodes
- multi-agent connections
- translucent topology lines
- data flows and light waves
- AI brain/chip symbols
- security shields
- light geometric accents

Do not use dense UI dashboards, repeated pill controls, glossy skeuomorphism, heavy neon, thick borders, or excessive decoration.

## Composition System

Use open white space, one primary composition per page, and restrained blue-purple structures. Route diagrams, evidence pages, schedules, and outcomes to their appropriate page types instead of repeating a generic card grid. Repeat shared hierarchy, margins, and color semantics in every prompt within the same `layout_group`.

## Section Divider Template

Use a large blue-purple gradient number on the left, Chinese section name at a fixed shared position, small uppercase English subtitle, one-line bridge, a pale topic illustration on the right, and a subtle blue-purple wave along the bottom.

## Titleless Mode

The title whitespace must be uninterrupted white background with no visible container. Body content begins below it. Do not mention or draw a “title box.”

## Negative Constraints

Avoid large dark backgrounds, dense dashboards, repeated pill controls, glossy skeuomorphism, heavy neon, thick borders, excessive decoration, and invented institutional or research claims. Re-generate a noncompliant image instead of moving, cropping, resizing, padding, or extending it.
